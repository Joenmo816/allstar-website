### Quick ID
- Small tan roach with two dark stripes
- Activity in warm, greasy, cluttered areas

### Risks
Allergens, food contamination, rapid population growth

### Prevention
Declutter/clean, bag trash nightly, fix leaks

### Professional Treatment
Bait/IGR program, targeted crack/crevice, sanitation coaching, rechecks
