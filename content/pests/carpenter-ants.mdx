### Quick ID
- Large ants; sawdust-like **frass**; winged swarmers in spring.

### Risks
Structural weakening as galleries expand in moist wood.

### Prevention
Fix leaks and moisture, trim vegetation, seal gaps.

### Professional Treatment
Inspection → targeted perimeter/void applications → moisture corrections → follow-up.
