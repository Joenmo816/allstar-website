### Quick ID
- Carrot-shaped, silvery with 3 tail filaments
- Found in bathrooms, attics, boxes

### Risks
Damages paper, books, fabrics

### Prevention
Dehumidify; store off floor; seal gaps

### Professional Treatment
Targeted crack/crevice and void treatments; monitor & follow-up
