### Quick ID
- Fast-flying yellow/black wasps
- Nests in ground, wall voids, shrubs

### Risks
Painful stings; swarm defense; allergy risk

### Prevention
Keep lids tight; seal entry voids; avoid vibrations near nests

### Professional Treatment
Protective-gear removal + targeted residues/aerosols; late-day service best
