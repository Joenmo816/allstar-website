### Quick ID
- Bites in rows/clusters; black spotting on seams
- Found in beds, couches, baseboards

### Risks
Bites, anxiety, hitchhiking in belongings

### Prevention
Reduce clutter; bag laundry; limit secondhand items

### Professional Treatment
Inspection, heat/chemical program, encasements, follow-up monitoring
