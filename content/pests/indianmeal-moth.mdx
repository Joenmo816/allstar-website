### Quick ID
- Small moths with two-tone wings flying in zig-zag
- Larvae/webbing in opened packages

### Risks
Food loss/contamination

### Prevention
Find and discard infested items; store in airtight containers

### Professional Treatment
Source removal + pheromone monitoring; targeted residuals if needed
