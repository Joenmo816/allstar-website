### Quick ID
- Burrows under slabs/woodpiles; greasy rub marks
- Large droppings; gnaw damage

### Risks
Property damage, contamination, disease concerns

### Prevention
Remove harborage; secure trash; fix drainage

### Professional Treatment
Exterior bait program + exclusion, trap inside as needed, follow-up
