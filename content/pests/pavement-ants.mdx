### Quick ID
- Fine soil mounds along sidewalks/driveways
- Steady trails to kitchen/hard floors

### Risks
Food contamination; nuisance trails

### Prevention
Sweep mounds; seal cracks; tighten sanitation

### Professional Treatment
Exterior perimeter treatment + interior baiting; crack/crevice work
