### Quick ID
- Large roach with yellowish margin behind head
- Seen in basements/mechanical rooms

### Risks
Contamination concerns from drains/sewers

### Prevention
Seal gaps; drain covers; improve airflow/dryness

### Professional Treatment
Targeted residuals, gel baits, drain treatments, mechanical exclusion
