### Quick ID
- Uniform tan with dark violin on cephalothorax
- Found in closets, basements, storage

### Risks
Occasional medically important bites

### Prevention
Reduce clutter; wear gloves when moving boxes; seal gaps

### Professional Treatment
Inspection, targeted crack/void treatments, sticky monitors, follow-up
