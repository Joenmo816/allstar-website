### Quick ID
- Varied-pattern adult beetles; fuzzy larvae
- Shed larval skins near baseboards/closets

### Risks
Damage to natural fibers/stored items

### Prevention
Launder/clean; store fabrics airtight; vacuum edges/under furniture

### Professional Treatment
Targeted crack/crevice + source cleanup; inspection for nests
