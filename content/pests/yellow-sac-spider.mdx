### Quick ID
- Cream to yellow; builds small silk sacs along ceilings/corners.

### Risks
Occasional irritating bites; usually mild.

### Prevention
Reduce clutter/webs, seal gaps, manage outdoor lighting.

### Professional Treatment
Crack/crevice applications, web removal, entry exclusion, monitoring.
