### Quick ID
- Perfectly round 1/2` holes in fascia/rails
- Large bee resembling bumble bee; shiny abdomen

### Risks
Cosmetic/structural wood damage over time

### Prevention
Paint/seal exposed wood; plug old holes in late fall

### Professional Treatment
Treat galleries + dust/plug; advise painting/sealing to prevent re-use
