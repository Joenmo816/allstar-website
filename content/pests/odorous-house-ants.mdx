### Quick ID
- Small brown ants forming trails to sweets
- Nests under stones/mulch, inside walls

### Risks
Food contamination; persistent trailing indoors

### Prevention
Seal entry points; trim vegetation; store sweets airtight

### Professional Treatment
Inspection, targeted gel/baiting + exterior band; source/moisture corrections
