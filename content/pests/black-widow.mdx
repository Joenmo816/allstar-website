### Quick ID
- Tangled webs under benches, eaves, meter boxes
- Distinctive red hourglass on underside

### Risks
Medically important bites (seek medical advice)

### Prevention
Knock down webs; gloves when working outdoors; reduce harborage

### Professional Treatment
Careful removal + targeted residuals around harborage, safety-first protocol
